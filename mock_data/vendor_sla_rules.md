# Enterprise Vendor SLA Master Registry

## Corporate Section: GlobalTech Partners
Standard parts shipments must arrive within 5 business days. Any continuous delay beyond this window incurs a standard operational fine of $1,000 USD per day of recorded breach.

## Corporate Section: ApexLogistics Global
In the event that delivery of critical hardware components (including semiconductor assets) is delayed past 7 business days, ApexLogistics shall be penalized a flat rate of $15,000 USD per day of recorded delay. All escalation notices must be delivered to the Senior Logistics Director within 15 days of breach.

## Corporate Section: Nexus Freight Systems
Logistics windows maintain a grace parameter of 3 business days. Delays exceeding 3 business days are billed a penalty tariff of $3,500 USD per afternoon of outstanding inventory arrival stagnation.
