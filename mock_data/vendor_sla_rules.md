# ApexLogistics Global - Master Service Agreement (SLA)
## Section 4.2: Delivery Timelines & Liquidated Damages
In the event that delivery of critical hardware components (including semiconductor assets) is delayed past 7 business days, ApexLogistics shall be penalized a flat rate of $15,000 USD per day of recorded delay. 
All escalation notices must be delivered to the Senior Logistics Director within 15 days of breach.
